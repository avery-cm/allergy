Meet the Allergies History
History
1996
Avery discovers her first Allergy, Miss Eggs d’Omelet, in a piece of cake. Her second Allergy, Senor Seafood, quickly follows after she accidentally eats salmon and her lip swells up to Kylie Jenner proportions.
1998
Backup allergies are included on the spring tour, including Kitty Kat, Mister Pollen, and Dust.
2018
Avery makes her worldwide debut in San Francisco.
1997
The Allergies Sextet forms: Miss Eggs, Senor Seafood, Nana Nuts, Beefy Boi, and the B’nana & Kiwi Duo.
2000
Avery takes her Allergies on tour to Berkeley. Thanks to Berkeley's whole vegan thing, she finds that Nana Nuts is the most welcome, but Miss Eggs, Senor Seafood, and Beefy Boi are not greeted as kindly.
